// Definition of vector lexemes

Digit:          `0`...`9`
Char:           `A`...`Z` |
                `a`...`z` |
                `_`
Num:            Digit+
Arith-sym:      `+`|`-`|`/`|`%`|`*`|`^`
Comp-sym:       `<`|`>`|`==`|`<=`|`>=`|`!=`
Aug-sym:        `+=`|`-=`|
Id:             Char (Char|Num)
Simp-Type:      Id
Array-Type:     Simp-Type (`[` [Num] `]`)*
Dict-Type:      Simp-Type `[` Simp-Type `,` (Simp-Type|Array-Type) `]`
Type:           Simp-Type|Array-Type|Dict-Type
